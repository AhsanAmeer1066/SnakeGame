RULES: SNAKE GAME
1. If the snake hits the walls of the window you lose.
2. If the snake hits it's own body you lose.
3. Eat the fruits to increase the size of the snake and increase your score.
4. In multiplayer, the last snake standing wins.
5. The blue snake is player 1, and is controlled by arrow keys.
6. The red snake is player 2, and is controlled by WASD keys.
